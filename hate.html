Do Hate Better

I hate semen...

Present day technologies are advancing; contraceptives and sperm donation are allowing people to control reproduction and bodily fluids in new and more advanced ways than ever before. Could this be taken a step further, where devices designed to capture and contain semen are optimised and used to maximise efficiency in clinics, bedrooms and beyond? How far will our semen-storing selves take this endeavour?



















May 2019

Do Hate Better

As a development to this project, I began drawing illustrations on different types of semen and sperm, depending on what context it might exist in. The idea was to raise awareness to the different types of semen and sperm we might encounter, what they are worth, and provoke healthy discussions that could result in better emotional experiences and relationships with semen.

This resulted in production of a pack of Top Trumps cards that could then be used to start these discussions. There are blank spaces to allow players to discuss and debate their thoughts on certain aspects.
